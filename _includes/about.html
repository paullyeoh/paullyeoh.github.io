About

PAUL L. YEOH (he/him) has a Ph.D. in Literatures in English from Rutgers University, where he specialized in Romantic and Victorian literature. When not writing, he works for an educational publishing company and teaches part-time at the University of Nebraska. Although based in the United States, Paul grew up in Southeast Asia and maintains close ties with the region through family

and friends in Singapore and Malaysia. He identifies as Chindian, a portmanteau term that acknowledges his Chinese and Indian parentage. Paul lives with his husband Dario in Omaha, NE, and is currently completing his first novel, a queer romantasy inspired by European and Southeast Asian folklore.
